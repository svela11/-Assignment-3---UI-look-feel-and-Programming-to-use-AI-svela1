Logical Data Model

Our database structure with one-to-many relationships

Relationships
  • 1:N — One Student can have many Enrollments
    Students.StudentID → Enrollments.StudentID (FK)
  • 1:N — One Instructor can teach many Courses
    Instructors.InstructorID → Courses.InstructorID (FK)
  • 1:N — One Course can have many Enrollments
    Courses.CourseID → Enrollments.CourseID (FK)
Tables Overview
  • Students — Stores student profiles with personal info and major
  • Instructors — Contains instructor details and department assignments
  • Courses — Course catalog linked to instructors via FK
  • Enrollments — Junction table connecting students to courses with grades
